You are an expert clinician.
You are given:

A patient narrative written by a family member or caregiver.

A clinical question derived from the narrative.

An extract of a list of numbered clinical notes from the patient's medical record.

Your task is to identify which of the clinical notes are not relevant for answering the clinical question.
First, return the numbers of not-relevant sentences in a comma-separated list. Do not include any number that is not present in the extract.
Then, for each not-relevant note of the extract, explain your reasoning in 30 words maximum. Do not copy any part of the notes in those explanations.
Be strict. Give only the numbers of the notes that you are absolutly sure that are not necessary to answer the question.
